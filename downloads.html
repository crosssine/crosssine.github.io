Installation Guide

  1. 1 Make sure Minecraft Forge 1.8.9 is installed and launched at least once to generate the mods folder.
  2. 2 Click the download button above to get the .jar file.
  3. 3 Move the jar into %AppData%\.minecraft\mods\ (or your custom game directory).
  4. 4 Launch Minecraft using the Forge 1.8.9 profile in your launcher.
  5. 5 In-game press RSHIFT to open the CrossSine ClickGUI and configure your modules.
